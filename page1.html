Medium Yellow

It is a wonderful onion with a single center. The home cook, as well, the experienced chef will appreciate this onion. They are 2 1/4 to 3 1/4 inches in size!

The Haun Packing Medium Yellow Onions are shipped in a 50 lb and 25 lb bag under the Honey Sweet label. They can also be shipped in a 40 lb box. 

Medium Red

They are a rich red onion that is perfect for gourmet cooking, salad bars, and a sweet addition to any burger! They are 2 1/4 to 3 1/4 inches and up in size!

These Medium Red onions are shipped in a 50 lb and 25 lb bag under the Royal Stuart label. They  can also be shipped in a 40 lb box.

Jumbo Yellow

It is a wonderful onion with a single center. The home cook, as well, the experienced chef will appreciate this onion. They are 3 inches and up in size!

The Haun Packing Jumbo Yellow Onions are shipped in a 50 lb and 25 lb bag under the Honey Sweet label. They can also be shipped in a 40 lb box. 

Jumbo Red

It is a sweet and flavorful raw onion. This onion works well as a large onion for the home cook and as a wonderfully sized onion for restaurants. The Haun Packing Jumbo Red onions are 3 inches and up in size!

The Jumbo Reds are shipped in a 50 lb and a 25 lb bag unser the H.O.C. label. They can also be shipped in a 40 lb box.

Collossal Yellow

These onions are a beautiful single centered copper brown color. They work very well for a variety of food service products. The Haun Packing Colossal Yellow Onions are 3 3/4  inches and up in size!

The Super Colossals are shipped in a 50 lb and 25 lb bag under the Royal Stuart label. They can also be shipped in a 40 lb box.
